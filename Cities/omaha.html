About Omaha

Omaha, or "Homaha" as some people call it is home of the College World Series each June. Located alongside the Missouri river, it posts a large population and has a diverse workforce.

5 Random Facts About Omaha:

  1. City Population: 500,000
  2. Incorperated: 1855
  3. Classification: Suburban
  4. Average Income Level Compared to rest of state: Higher than other cities in Nebraska ($72,000)
  5. Region: Eastern Nebraska
